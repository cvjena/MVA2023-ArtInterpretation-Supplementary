Anthony Van Dyck was a skillful portrait painter who was highly sought after by the nobility of his day. It is likely that he painted this self-portrait to demonstrate his skills to potential clients. It is a finely detailed painting, with the artist wearing a colorful waistcoat and striped stockings. His face is smooth and refined, with a hint of a smile. It is possible to see in his eyes the intelligence and sophistication that made him so successful as a portrait painter. Van Dyck is depicted wearing a fine silk dress, which sets the tone for the painting's realism. This lavish setting gives the impression of opulence and wealth.
