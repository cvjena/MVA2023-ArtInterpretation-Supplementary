The flowers in this painting are a beautiful mix of shades of pink, yellow, and white. They seem to be holding up their petals very proudly, as if they are standing up to the harsh light of the day. The chrysanthemums are surrounded by a bright, cheerful yellow sunflower, which seems to be bursting with life. The background is a mix of soft, creamy colors, making the flowers and their surroundings look like they are floating in a cloud. This painting is definitely a sight to behold, and it is definitely a great example of the Dutch Golden Age painting style.   There are many, many possible interpretations of this painting, as it is incredibly intricate and colorful.
