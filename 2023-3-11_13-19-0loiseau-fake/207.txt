This painting is a colorful, kinetic abstraction. It could represent movement or energy. The bright colors are overall cheerful and invite the viewer in. The various shapes and designs suggest movement and activity. The painting may also depict the artist's own thoughts and emotions at the time. It could be interpreted as a commentary on the importance of social norms and the role of women in society. Some possible interpretations could be that the woman is imagining themselves in the future, or that she is looking forward to the future, celebrating all of the progress women have made in society. The bright colors could also suggest that the woman is happy and content with her life. The artist's signature is infectious and lively.
