The painting portrays a vibrant and busy street in the center of Moscow. Colorful roses and blooms of the flowers cover the ground, while people of all social statuses can be seen walking and talking. The angular lines and shapes of the buildings and people create a dynamic and active impression. The vibrancy of the painting is reminiscent of the vibrancy and energy of the Russian Revolution. Serov was interested in the power and effects of light and shadow on the surface of things, and the energy of the painting reflects that. The painting could be seen as a representation of the way the Russian people felt and felt about their lives at the time. It could be seen as a celebration of life and of the beauty of the Russian Empire.
