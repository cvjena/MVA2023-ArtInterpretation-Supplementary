The Seventh Circle is a painting depicting the eternal punishment of hell. The demons inscribed in the painting are shown wrestling with each other for possession of a man, who is represented by a human skeleton. Satan himself stands at the center of the painting, with a set of horns protruding from his forehead. This is an iconic painting that is still used in graphic design and advertising today.  There are many possible interpretations to these paintings; the interpretations given are just a few examples.
