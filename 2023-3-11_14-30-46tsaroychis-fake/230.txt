Durer created this image to titillate, as in the figure to the right hand is gazing suggestively at a cup in his hand and the drapery flounces suggestively around his hip. The other figures seem to be looking on inattentively or are engaged in amorous activities. This painting is a piece of art that is intended to provoke and captivate the viewer. It could be interpreted as a depiction of a moment from the Bible in which Christ is being influenced by the devil. The dark, rich colors and the highly detailed clothing make this an arresting work.
