This abstract painting is composed of bold, intersecting lines that create a swirling dynamic. There is no specific subject or figure that is focal point, instead the painting is about the energy and force of movement. It could be interpreted as a depiction of the struggles of life, and the how everything is constantly in flux. The muted colors give the impression of depth and an underlying sense of turmoil. This painting is a revolutionary work, and its influence can be seen in many different forms of art. It is a reminder that art is always evolving and transforming, and that we can never be sure of it being the last thing on the canvas.
