This painting displays the spirit of Christ inspiring people to find and love their trials. The rainbow sign in the background represents the life-giving power of love. The angels and demons in the crowd are a reminder of the trials that we all experience. The soft light and delicate details in the painting give it a feeling of peace and beauty. It is a reminder that we must fight against the temptations of the world. The bold colors and sense of depth in the background suggest that there is much more to life than what can be found in the world of darkness. The sign above suggests that there is much more to life than what can be found in the world of money.
