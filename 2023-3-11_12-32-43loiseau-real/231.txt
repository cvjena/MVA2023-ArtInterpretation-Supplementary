 hops smashed in a flower pot.  This painting is a homage to Bonnard, who was known for his lush lush paintings of flowers. The oranges, yellows, and pinks in the flowers are meant to represent the sun's rays and the warm colors of the wine. The arranged flowers are meant to represent the earth and the flowers in a vase. Bonnard was known for his intense and powerful paintings of flowers. This painting is a great example of his lush lush paintings. The painting looks very fresh and colorful. The flowers in the vase represent the natural beauty of the flowers. The trees in the background are a source of shade and comfort. The painting is very peaceful and calming.
