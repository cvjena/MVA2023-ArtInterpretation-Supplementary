This painting features the Holy Family- Saint Joseph, Saint John the Baptist, and Saint Anne- looking on with fatherly pride at their infant son, John the Baptist. It is likely that this painting, with its religiously charged subject matter, was meant to be seen in a church. The father figure, Saint Joseph, is typically depicted in religious paintings as a very earnest and dutiful man, strictly following the rules of his faith. Saint Anne, the mother of the family, is usually depicted as a pious and wise woman. Together, they are a803lkd providing a model for pious Christian families. The son, John the Baptist, is depicted as a holy and fearless individual, foretelling the coming of Jesus Christ.
