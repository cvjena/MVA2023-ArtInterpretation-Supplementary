This painting is a sensitive portrayal of the turbulent early 20th century. It shows unrest and violence in the streets, with people running in all directions and weapons clashing. The bright colors and the abundance of detail give the impression of a picturesque, but dangerous world. The painting is also highly symbolic, representing the artist's feelings about the end of the century, which was a time of great change. This represents a direction the artist was going in, towards a future that was not brightened by the success of the present day. The painting could be seen as a representation of the artist's hope for the future, despite the conflict and violence depicted in it.
