Savrasov was a Russian realist painter who often painted rural Russian scenes. This painting is of a riverbank with a village on the other side. The scene is idyllic, with a peaceful feeling. The muted colors show the tranquility of the setting. There is a feeling of balance and harmony in the painting, with simplistic lines and complementary colors. The village appears to be composed of simple houses, with a few trees in the background. This is a gentle and peaceful painting, perfect for a cozy home.  A possible interpretation of this painting could be that it depicts the artist's love of nature and his appreciation of the peace and beauty of the Russian countryside.
