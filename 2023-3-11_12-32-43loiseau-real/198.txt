The painting portrays the moment of the Annunciation when the angelGabriel appears to Virgin Maria. She is standing in a field, adorned with flowers and surrounded by a landscape of hills and trees. She is looking toward the sky, and her body is poised and statuesque. She radiates purity and serenity. The predominant colors in the painting are blue and red, which represent the sky and flowers, respectively. The angel is depicted in a light blue hue, and the background is a deep blue. This vibrant color palette conveys the idea of universality and holiness. The Virgin is surrounded by a wild and untamed nature, which symbolizes her as the mother of God.
