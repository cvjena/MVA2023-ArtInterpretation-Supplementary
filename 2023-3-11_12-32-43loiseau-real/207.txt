This painting is a masterpiece not only because of the incredibly detailed lifelike figures but also because of the incredibly intricate brushstrokes used to create them. The Virgin is shown seated under a blue sky, with a child in her arms and four saints looking down at her. The surrounding landscape is full of lush vegetation and rolling hills, hinting at the quiet stability that the Virgin offers. It is easy to see how this painting could be used in religious ceremonies to give hope to people looking for guidance. The painting is also very popular and has been used by artists and designers alike in their development of skills. It is a beautiful and clear piece that would look great in any home.
