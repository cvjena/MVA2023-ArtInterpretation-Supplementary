This painting is an interpretation of the Bible story of the Annunciation, in which the angel Gabriel arrives to announce to the virgin Mary that she will be pregnant and bear Jesus. The painting features a central scene in which the angel proclaims to the Virgin Mary that she will be the mother of Jesus. Around the edges of the painting are details, including the Virgin'scloth and the sky, which is filled with a bright, blue halo. In the foreground, a small group of people watch the angel communicate with him.   The possible interpretation of this painting could be that the angel is announcing the beginning of God's plan to populate the world.
