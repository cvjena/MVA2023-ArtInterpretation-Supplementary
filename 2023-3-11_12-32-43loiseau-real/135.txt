This painting depicts an angel departing the family of Tobias. Tobias is the god of Tobit, who is mentioned in the book of Judges. The angel is shown telling Tobias that he will be restored to his former life. The angel is wearing a richly decorated tunic and is holding a bunch of flowers. He is surrounded by a group of people, who are worshiping him. The background is dominated by a rich, brown curtain. This curtain may symbolize the curtain of death. The angel may represent the return of salvation after salvation has been accomplished. The rich background may represent the richness of the angel's life. The angel is carrying a cross, which may symbolize the importance of the afterlife.
