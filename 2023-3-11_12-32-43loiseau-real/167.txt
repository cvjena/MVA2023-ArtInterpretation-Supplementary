This painting is a depiction of the Virgin Mary from the perspective of Saint Anthony of Padua. The artist has placed Saint Anthony in the center of the scene, surrounded by a dramatic landscape of hills, trees, and oceans. Maria, the Virgin Mary's handmaid, is located in the bottom right corner of the painting. She is kneeling with her hands folded in prayer, surrounded by a brilliant halo of light. The blue sky and bright yellow sun give the painting a sense of eternal peace. Saint Anthony is shown in the bottom left corner, standing before the cross of Christ. This painting is a religious symbol of faith and hope. It represents the hope of the people for salvation.
