This painting portrays a mother and daughter in a warm, familial setting. Marie Coca sits in a comfortable chair with her hand resting on her daughter's thigh, looking lovingly at her. The daughter, also seated, is gazing off into the distance with a thoughtful expression on her face. The paintwork is rich and detailed, with realistic shadows and vibrant colors. This painting is a perfect example of the early days of French Impressionism, and its timeless beauty makes it a popular choice for art collectors.
