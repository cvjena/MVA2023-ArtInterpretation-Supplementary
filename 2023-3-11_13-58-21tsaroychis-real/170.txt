This oil on canvas painting displays the likeness of a bearded man with striking green eyes. His skin is of a light, pale complexion, and his hair is a dark brown. He is wearing a sober brown suit with ruffled white shirt and a white tie. His physique is strong, and his hand is rested on his lap. He seems confident, dignified, and restrained. This painting is a portrayal of Prince G G Orlov, a prominent Russian statesman and diplomat in the late 1700s and early 1800s. Prince G G Orlov was a significant figure during the reign of Empress Catherine the Great, and played an important role in the development of Russian architecture and art.
