This painting is influenced by Italian masterLeonardo da Vinci. In this piece, the poet Dante and his beloved Beatrice are walking through the gates of heaven. Beatrice is wearing a beautiful red dress and Dante is wearing a white tunic and cloak. They are transfixed with love as they gaze at each other. Behind them is a golden city with a shining angel on top. This sublime painting is full of life and vitality. It is a testament to the power of love.  There are many interpretations that could be put on "Be With Me", but one could also interpret the painting in different ways.
