The Madonna and Child is a timeless masterpiece that depicts the love between a mother and her child. The saintly mother with her serene and pure facial expression is paired with the cherubic child, who is portrayed as innocent and pure. The child's innocent features and delicate features give the painting a youthful appeal that underscores the maternal love. This painting is a perfect example of the spiritual love that is at the heart of every human relationship.  A possible interpretation of "Madonna and Child" could be that the artist portrays the maternal love between a mother and their child as a symbol of purity and innocence.
