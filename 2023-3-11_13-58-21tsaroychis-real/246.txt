This painting is about a person who is attempting to learn from a teacher, who is appearing to be very old and experienced. She is sitting with her legs neatly crossed, with a soft and sad expression. This painting could be interpreted as a commentary on the life of a nun or a teacher, and how they struggle to learn and grow while suppressing their own emotions. The contrasting colors and soft, melancholy atmosphere of the painting create a sense of nostalgia and melancholy, which lends a feeling of sadness and introspection to the subject matter. The background is dominated by a deep, dark blue, which subtly references the depths of the ocean.
