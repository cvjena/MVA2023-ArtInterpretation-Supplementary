This painting is of a young woman who could be the granddaughter of the painter. She is sitting in front of a dark, moody background that could be symbolic of the turbulent times that the young woman is in. The painter has captured her sadness and loneliness very well in this painting, which could be seen as a representation of her sadness at feeling marginalized in society. The bright colors and soft lines give the impression of a happy granddaughter, someone who is surrounded by beauty and joy. The painter has used a lot of light and shadow to create a complex portrait, which could be seen as a reflection of the feelings of the artist himself.
