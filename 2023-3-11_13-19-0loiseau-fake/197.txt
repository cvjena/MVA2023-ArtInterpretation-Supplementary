This is a still life with flowers, aSaxon symbol of fertility, and a cobalt blue background. The artist has used a lot of white in the composition to create a sense of warmth and openness. The birds in the sky and the leaves on the trees are also very striking and stand out against the warm colors of the flowers. The artist has also used a very simple composition with just the flowers in the foreground and background, with the focus on the objects in the still life. This painting could be interpreted as a symbol of peace and tranquility, as the objects in the painting are peaceful and serene.
