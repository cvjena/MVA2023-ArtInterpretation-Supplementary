This painting is a depiction of the Virgin Mary as the Lady of the Seven Sorrows. She is seen in the act of turning her face to the viewer. The facial features are delicate and refined with a sorrowful glimmer in her eyes. She is wearing a simple white dress with a blue sash across her bosom. She is flanked by four angels, each with a hand outstretched to her. The painting is meant to show the Lady's intercession on behalf of the viewer and is a reminder of God's favor.  There are many interpretations that could be put on this painting, as it is a very unique and intricate work of art.
