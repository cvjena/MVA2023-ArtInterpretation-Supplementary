This painting is a depiction of an undulating field of a rainbow. Bright, azure, and velvety sky is used to create a sense of the mystical. The painting is reminiscent of the West's days of being a land of beauty and change. The rainbow is a symbol of hope, love, and light, and its colors are a tribute to the beauty of nature. Roerich often painted scenes of nature in a cheerful or romantic atmosphere, and this painting is no exception. The seemingly idyllic scene is full of light and motion, and is a perfect depiction of the radiant beauty of the East.  There are many possible interpretations for any given painting.
