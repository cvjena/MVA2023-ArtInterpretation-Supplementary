In this painting, the viewer is looking at the sea from a small mound called "L Estaque." The sun is behind the Mirabeau mountain and casts a diffused light on the sea. There is a ship at the horizon and a few smaller boats in the distance. The waves are gentle and have a creamy color. The background is a light blue and the foreground is a dark blue.    viewer looking out to the ocean various boats in the distance, including a ship a backdrop of the Mirabeau mountain a light blue sea with waves that are gentle and have a creamy color.
