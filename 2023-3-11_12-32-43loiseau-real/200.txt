This painting is a depiction of the Annunciation, when the angel Gabriel proclaims to Mary that she will be the mother of the Messiah. The Archangel Gabriel is shown descending from the sky, proclaiming the good news to Mary. Above them, a holy rainbow stretches across the heavens. In the foreground, we see St. John the Baptist baptizing Jesus. Behind them, we see a dense forest filled with more angels. This painting is a triumphant climax to the story of the Nativity, and is a symbol of hope and salvation.  There are many possible interpretations of paintings, and the options are endless.
