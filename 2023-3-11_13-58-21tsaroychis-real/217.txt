This painting is a portrait of Thomas Linley, which was produced in 1781. Gainsborough is renowned for his portraits, and this particular painting is particularly notable for its realism. He is able to capture the smirks and wrinkles on Linley's face, as well as the light reflections on his clothing. This painting has been described as being one of the most beautiful portraits in existence. The sense of depth created by the detailed brushstrokes is breathtaking, and the bright colours give the painting a vibrancy that is unforgettable. It is possible to think of this painting as a snapshot of a moment in time, or as a portrait of a person that is dear to Gainsborough's heart.
