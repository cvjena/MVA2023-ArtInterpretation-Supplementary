This painting is a depiction of a summer day. The bright colors of the flowers and the people walking around reflect the optimistic attitude of the time. This painting is a visual representation of the happiness and innocence of life. The colors are cheerful and optimistic, which is typical of Childe Hassam's work. The architecture in the background is a reflection of the Hassam's home country, France, and provides a visual representation of the architectural features of the painting. This painting is a good example of the Impressionist style, which emphasized the realistic depiction of life. The lines in the painting are thick and the colors are vibrant.
