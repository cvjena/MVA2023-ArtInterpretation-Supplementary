This painting is of two cowherds by a river. The landscape is peaceful and pastoral, with smooth, rolling hills in the background. The cowherds are lazily grazing their cows, and the water is shining brightly reflecting the sunlight. This painting is a beautiful example of the Dutch Golden Age style, and it would be perfect for a home with a garden. The cowherds could symbolize the peacefulness and placor of rural life, and they could be a representation of the Dutch Republic itself, which was once a major Empire of the world.
