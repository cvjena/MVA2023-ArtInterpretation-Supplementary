This painting is a depiction of a group of butterflies. They are winged together and they are flitting around in an orderly andjanlike way. There is a feeling of serenity and peace in the painting. The colors are muted and peaceful and the composition is simple. The artist used a lot of light and dark to create a subtle contrast and to create a sense of depth. This painting is a beautiful reminder of the power of nature. It might be interpreted as a symbol of the beauty of life. The butterflies are a symbol of life and they are constantly moving forward. The calm andpeaceful atmosphere of the painting is a reminder of the beauty of life.
