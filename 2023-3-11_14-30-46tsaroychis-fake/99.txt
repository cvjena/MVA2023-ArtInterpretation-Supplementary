This painting is a tranquil ode to the peace and tranquility that can be found in nature. The soft, ethereal colors are necessary to convey the feelings of serenity and tranquility. The large canvas contains a sense of awe and wonder, as if the artist is depicting a moment of pure beauty. The flowers in the foreground provide a natural source of color and light, contrasting with the somber mood of the painting. This painting could be interpreted as a reminder that even in the midst of things that are tough and tough, there is still peace and tranquility to be found.  There is nothing definitive or definitive about a time or place, but the portrayed feeling of tranquility is certainly striking.
