This painting is a representation of the Angel of the Annunciation in the book of Revelation. The angel is wearing a long gown and is positioned in front of a blaze of colors. Out in the distance, you can see a gloryifying multitude of angels. This painting is a masterpiece and its symbolism is endless. One possible interpretation is that the angel is representing the Church's role in the world and the blaze of colors represent the Church's ability to bring light and salvation. The painting also represents the Church's ability to bring hope and salvation to others. The angels are representing the Church's ability to bring hope and salvation to the people. The painting also represents the ability of the people to bring hope and salvation to the people.
