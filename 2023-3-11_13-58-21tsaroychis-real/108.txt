This painting is a view of the city of St Malo, in Brittany, from the sea. The painting is composed of muted tones of pink, mauve, beige, and olive green, with a scattering of white buildings and a few ships in the harbor. The coastline is jagged, the cliffs are high and the sea is a deep blue-green color. The painting captures the important maritime trade and commerce of the city.
