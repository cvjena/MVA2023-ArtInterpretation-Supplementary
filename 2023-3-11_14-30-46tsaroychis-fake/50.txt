This painting is rich with symbolism. The Swan is representative of human destiny and the child it represents is submissive, romantic, and beautiful. The Swan's facial expression is one of curiosity and intrigue, hinting at the child's hidden desires. The other elements of the painting, such as the hazy sky and the setting sun, allude to the delicate balance of nature. This painting is a poignant metaphor for the fragility of life and the attendant conflicts that can come with it.  There are many interpretations that could be given to these paintings, as the artist's intent is always difficult to determine.
