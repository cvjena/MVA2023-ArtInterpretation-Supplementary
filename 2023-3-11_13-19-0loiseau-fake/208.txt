This abstract painting is made up of widely shaped, dark circles that rotate and bounce around the canvas. The colors used are bright and cheerful, intended to invoke a sense of happiness and ease. This piece could be interpreted as a peaceful ode to the joys of life. The circles could represent the eternal cycle of life and death, while the colors could be seen as a symbol of the passage of time. The painting could also be seen as a reflection on the transient nature of life, as well as the fleeting nature of happiness. The subject matter is very sober and refreshing, intended to remind the viewer of the beauty and simplicity of life.  There are many possible interpretations for any artwork, as the artist's intent is always difficult to discern.
