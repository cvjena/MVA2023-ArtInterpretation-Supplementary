This painting is a view of the bustling port town of Le Havre in France. The bright colors of the buildings and the boats create a lively picture. The high cliffs in the background add a excitement to the scene. The cliffs could be a reference to the ocean, or they could simply be the scenery of the town. Theight sky and the warm colors of the buildings give the painting a sense of peace. The painting could also be interpreted as a picture of the joys of life in the town. The people in the town appear to be enjoying themselves, and the flowers in the trees are a beautiful addition to the background. Le Havre is a beautiful town, and this painting captures its beauty.
