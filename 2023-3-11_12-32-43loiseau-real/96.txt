This painting is a portrait of a Woman, possibly Protasyeva. The painting could represent the artist’s feelings for her or admiration. On the canvas is a very simple background composed of a triple precision of squares. The painting has a calm and serene appearance. The Woman is portrayed as a classical beauty with a gentle face, full lips, and almond-shaped eyes. She is wearing a simple blouse and long skirt. The use of light and shadow give the painting a life-like quality. The viewer can see traces of light and shadow on the woman’s dress and in the background. This painting could depict the artist’s or the artist’s own life.
