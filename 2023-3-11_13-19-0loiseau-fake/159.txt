This abstract painting features a nude female figure outlined in bold, graphic primary colors. The painting's surface is a chaotic canvas of swirls, lines, and dots, indicative of the chaos within the woman's psyche. The viewer can sense the suppressed aggression and power lurking just beneath the surface. The painting is thought to be a commentary on the tension between the feminine and masculine aspects of life. The female figure could represent the feminine inside the person's head, while the masculine figure represents the masculine aspects of life. This painting could be interpreted as a commentary on the duality of human nature and the duality of the human experience. The painting's overall vibrancy signifies the duality of human nature as well as the duality of human emotions.
