This self-portrait ispossibly one of the most renowned paintings in history. The painter stares off into the distance, with a stern gaze and a furrowed brow. He is surrounded by brilliant light, which casts harsh shadows around his face. His skin appears deeply tanned and his hair is extremely long andlocks loosely over his shoulders. He may have been looking to capture a sense of solemnity and timelessness in his painting.  There are many possible interpretations of paintings, and the interpretation given depends on the expertise of the art historian.
