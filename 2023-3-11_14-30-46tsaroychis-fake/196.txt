This painting likely represents the Madonna and Child as a symbol of protection. The saint is symbolizing faith, while the rabbit may represent purity. The soft light swirling around the saint and the rabbit gives the painting a warm and welcoming feel. The Child looks up at the saint, who is looking out adoringly at him. The flowers that are arranged in front of the saint could represent the saint's holiness. The purity of the rabbits may symbolize the saint's pure emotions. This painting is a perfect depiction of the contrasts between good and evil in the world. The saint is surrounded by sinners, while the rabbits may represent the saint's loyal companions. The cheerful colors and the peaceful setting give the painting a feeling of warmth and peace.
